Our Story

Founded on a Single Complaint

Aureline began in 2013 after our founder, a frequent business traveler, missed a closing meeting because a "luxury" car service sent a driver without directions and no way to reach dispatch. We built the company we wished had existed that morning: fewer cars, better vetted, and a concierge team that actually picks up.

We started with four sedans and a single city. Every vehicle we've added since has had to earn its place in the fleet — passing the same inspection standard, driven by chauffeurs trained to the same quiet, exacting service. Growth was never the goal; consistency was. The fact that we now serve 42 cities is simply a byproduct of not lowering the bar.

Today, Aureline moves executives, wedding parties, touring artists, and diplomats — but the standard is identical for every one of them: the ride should be the calmest part of your day.
